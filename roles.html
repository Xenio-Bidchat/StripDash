User roles

Roles define what permissions are granted to team members in your Stripe account.

  • Administrator
    Best for business owners and company administrators
  • Developer
    Best for developers or people primarily using the Stripe API
  • Analyst
    Best for people who need full access to Stripe data, but don't need to update business settings
  • Support Specialist
    Best for employees who regularly refund payments and respond to disputes
  • View only
    Best for people who need to view Stripe data, but don't need to make any updates
